Mobile App Gas Booking Procedure

Follow these steps to book your gas through our mobile app:

  1. Download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Open the app and sign in to your account or register a new account.
  3. Select 'Book Gas' from the app's home screen.
  4. Enter your address and the number of cylinders you want to order.
  5. Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction.
  6. You will receive an in-app notification and SMS confirming your booking.

Once your order is processed, the gas will be delivered to your specified address at the scheduled time.
