SMS Gas Booking Procedure

Follow these steps to book your gas via SMS:

  1. Save our customer service number on your phone: +1234567890
  2. Send an SMS with the following format: "BOOK Gas
    "
  3. Once we receive your SMS, our team will confirm the details and process your order.
  4. You will receive a confirmation SMS with the expected delivery time.

If you face any issues, feel free to call our customer support number for assistance.
